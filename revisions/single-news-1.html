MANILA - Senator Francis Escudero has accepted the offer of Sen. Grace Poe to run for vice-president in the 2016 elections.

The lawmaker, who once dabbled with the idea of running for a higher position in 2010, will run as the vice-president of Grace Poe, who announced on Wednesday her decision to run for president.

The long-running friendship between the Poes and Escudero makes the latter the obvious choice for the lady senator, who is currently the front runner in the presidential race according to the pre-election surveys of Social Weather Stations and Pulse Asia.

The closeness of Poe and Escudero is what analysts and pundits believe derailed the Liberal Party's plan to pair Poe with former Interior Secretary Manuel Roxas II.
